About Project

Our project revolves around using Computer Vision to analyze soccer matches in real-time, employing YOLOv5 and ByteTracker methodologies. We're focusing on improving soccer video analysis by spotting players and the ball with precision, tracking their movements, and identifying game events swiftly. By tapping into YOLOv5's excellent object detection and ByteTracker's efficient tracking, our goal is to accurately recognize players and the ball, allowing quick analysis and creating game highlights. These advanced computer vision tools offer coaches and analysts comprehensive insights to optimize team strategies. Our work aims to advance sports analytics by providing a fast, accurate, and adaptable solution for real-time soccer match analysis.
